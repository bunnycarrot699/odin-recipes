World's best lasagna

This lasagna recipe may seem difficult, but belive me, it is worth the work!

Ingredients

  • Meat: 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • Onion: 1/2 cup
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoon
  • Spices and seasoning: salt, pepper, dried basil, chopped fresh parsley
  • Lasagna noodles: 12 pieces
  • Cheeses: 3/4 pound mozzarella, 3/4 cup grated Parmesan, 12 ounces ricotta
  • Egg: 1 piece
  • Tomato sauce: 1 can

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna: meat sauce - noodles - ricotta mixture - mozzarella slices - repeat
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
